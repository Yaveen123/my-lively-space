Privacy Policy

Overview

Anket is a new tab page extension that lets you sign in with your Google account so calendar events can be shown on your new tab page.

What data is used

Anket requests access to your basic Google profile and Google Calendar data needed to display your upcoming events.

How data is used

Your calendar data is used only to fetch and render your events in the Anket interface. It is not sold or used for advertising.

Storage and retention

Only data required for extension functionality is stored. If tokens or temporary event data are cached, they are kept only as long as needed to provide the service.

Your choices
